Our Work

02

Dell Technologies

We are a technology company that develop, sell, repair and support computers and related products and services. We have partnered with Soho House to manage their technical side of things so that they can continue to deliver exclusive offers to their members.

Soho House

We are a group of private member's clubs originally aimed at those in the arts, politics and media but we have since expanded and now operate clubs, hotels and venues around the world. As we have now partnered with Dell Technologies, we are now offering these exclusive venues and offers to their employees!

Inconnection

We are an events agency with decades of experience offering live, hybrid and virtual events to our clients. We are proud to be the driving force between Dell Technologies and Soho House and we look forward to the exciting events that are to come in the future!
